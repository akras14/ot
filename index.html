Los Angeles Occupational Therapy Services

Welcome to Los Angeles Occupational Therapy, where we are dedicated to providing personalized and effective therapy solutions to enhance your quality of life. Below is a list of the comprehensive services we offer:

Our Services

1. Pediatric Occupational Therapy

Helping children develop the necessary skills for daily activities, social interaction, and school performance.

  • Fine motor skills development
  • Sensory integration therapy
  • Handwriting improvement
  • Play therapy and social skills training

2. Adult Occupational Therapy

Assisting adults in regaining independence after injury, illness, or surgery.

  • Rehabilitation therapy
  • Ergonomic assessment and workplace modification
  • Daily living skills training
  • Hand therapy

3. Geriatric Occupational Therapy

Supporting seniors in maintaining independence and improving overall well-being.

  • Fall prevention strategies
  • Cognitive assessment and training
  • Energy conservation techniques
  • Adaptive equipment recommendations

4. Mental Health Occupational Therapy

Promoting mental wellness through meaningful activities and coping strategies.

  • Stress management techniques
  • Routine and role development
  • Community reintegration programs
  • Mindfulness and relaxation exercises

5. Home and Community-Based Services

Bringing therapy to the comfort of your own home or community setting.

  • Home safety evaluations
  • Community mobility and transportation training
  • Activity of daily living (ADL) assessments
  • Environmental adaptations and modifications
